# Career Fair Employer Landing Page Design Brief

## Goal
Create an refreshed club webpage including a new modern landing page for companies interested in joining a university career fair hosted by a graduate level Quantum Sciences Program with collaboration in Materials Sciences, Electrical Engineering, Computer Sciences, and Physics.

## Source Material
USe the information in the provided .csv files and the QSES_Webpage to populate the new site. The site should be a revamped version of the QSES Webpage with the new landing page.

## Audience
Recruiters, HR teams, technical hiring managers, corporate relations staff, and sponsor representatives.

## Desired style
Clean institutional SaaS style with similar formatting to other university career fair webpages, but with modern updates. The page should feel credible, polished, and easy to scan.

## Visual direction
- Off-white or white background
- Navy or charcoal text
- Yellow accent color
- Color scheme aligns with UDelaware_Employers.html
- Rounded cards
- Subtle borders and shadows
- Generous whitespace
- Minimal animations
- Professional but not boring
- Modern

## Required sections for new landing page
1. Hero with event name, date, location, and CTA
2. Audience statistics
3. Employer benefits
4. Employer package tiers
5. Schedule
6. Registration steps
7. FAQ
8. Final call-to-action

## Primary CTA
Register Your Company

## Secondary CTA
View Sponsorship Options

## Avoid
- Overly playful visuals
- Excessive gradients
- Hard-to-read dark forms
- Complicated animations
- Generic stock-photo-heavy design
