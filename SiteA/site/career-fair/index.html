FAQ

Common employer questions.

Which employers are a good fit?

Quantum-centric companies, big tech quantum divisions, defense employers, national labs, photonics companies, semiconductor companies, and technical suppliers are strong fits.

Can we attend if we are not hiring for quantum roles only?

Yes. The audience includes students with transferable skills in software, hardware, physics, devices, materials, controls, cryogenics, and engineering research.

Is the registration link live?

Employer registration is coming soon. Student Handshake and external Google Form registration links will also be posted when available.

Are national labs charged the same fee?

Government and nonprofit employers may register for $75, including admission for two representatives. Each additional representative is $25.
