Graduate student community for quantum science and engineering

Connecting quantum researchers, students, and employers at UD.

Our mission is to foster an inclusive, dynamic community that supports the academic, professional, and personal growth of Quantum Science and Engineering graduate students.

QSES advocates for member interests and well-being through consultation with department and university leadership. Through networking opportunities, skill-building workshops, and community events, QSES empowers emerging leaders, strengthens the quantum community, and supports holistic development for all members.
