Sponsorship Opportunities

Sponsorship Opportunities allow our employer partners greater opportunity to build their brand awareness among all University of Michigan Students. Are you...

  • Looking to connect with students across many majors and/or levels of study?
  • Trying to create a high level of visibility and brand recognition for your organization?
  • Interested in working with students from a wide range backgrounds and experiences?
  • Trying to get your message to students in a simple and effective way?

Our sponsorship packages offer you the chance to sponsor some of the most popular resources among both undergraduate and graduate students. These sponsorships are available on an annual basis, with an easy renewal option each academic year.

Premium Sponsor for our Clothes Closet | Exclusive to one employer per year | $8,000/annual

Premium Sponsor for our Professional Headshot Photo Booth | Exclusive to one employer per year | $2,500/annual

Premium Sponsor for our Interview Ready Room | Available to select employers annually | $1,000/annual

The UCC has select private interview room suites available for students and employers. Students with reservations or employer-hosted interviews also have access to our Interview Ready Room (refreshments, interviewing tips, last minute needs, printer, etc.) thanks to our generous employer sponsors. 

Connect with Top Talent at the University of Michigan: Explore Our Tiered Sponsorship Packages

At the University Career Center, we are proud to offer our Employer Partner Packages , designed to deepen your brand presence, enhance recruiting outcomes, and meaningfully engage with the next generation of talent. Our tiered sponsorship model—Maize, Blue, and Wolverine—offers customizable benefits tailored to your strategic recruitment priorities.

Why Partner with Us?

As a sponsor, you gain priority access to high-impact recruiting opportunities, including exclusive event participation, direct student engagement platforms, and strategic branding on campus and online. Whether you're seeking top interns, building long-term brand visibility, or refining your early talent strategy, our packages deliver intentional, high-touch engagement.

Partnership Tiers & Benefits

Wolverine Package – $8,000 
Our most comprehensive partnership, offering maximum visibility and access:

  • Premier presence at signature events such as Career Jam, Skilled & Savvy Conference, and Employer Challenge.
  • Co-host a student workshop and join our Employer Advisory Board.
  • Priority booth placement and waived fees at both in-person and virtual job fairs.
  • Premium digital branding and inclusion in specialized Handshake collections.
  • Strategic consultations with our leadership team and student focus groups.

Blue Package – $4,000 
A balanced approach for growing employer brands:

  • Engage in career development events and host on-campus or virtual sessions.
  • Feature in targeted newsletters and enjoy priority booth placement at fairs.
  • Access recruitment strategy sessions and a presence in our branding spaces.

Maize Package – $2,000 
A great starting point for visibility and relationship-building:

  • Participate in select events and receive recognition in Career Track communications.
  • Gain targeted exposure via curated marketing opportunities.

Explore full package details and benefits here. 

If you are interested in engaging in any of these sponsorship opportunities, let's connect! You can reach out to your respective recruitment coordinator here. We'd love to share details and data on how this could positively impact your recruiting efforts with Michigan students.
