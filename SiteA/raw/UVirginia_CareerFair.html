The UVA Career Center, UVA Enrollment and UVA's Provost office have created this program to expand meaningful work opportunities for students while supporting employers with access to motivated, high-potential talent. Through TFWS, employers receive a 75% wage subsidy, making it easier to bring FWS students on board.
